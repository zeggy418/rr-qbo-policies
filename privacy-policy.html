Privacy Policy — Ready Rebound QBO Integration

Ready Rebound, Inc.  |  Effective: April 20, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Ready Rebound, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Ready Rebound QBO Integration (the "Application"). This Application is an internal business tool used exclusively by authorized Ready Rebound, Inc. personnel to read financial data from the company's QuickBooks Online account.

This Application is not a consumer-facing product and is not available to the general public or to third-party users.

2. Information We Access

When authorized by an administrator of the Ready Rebound QuickBooks Online account, the Application retrieves the following data via the Intuit QuickBooks Online API:

  • Financial transactions and journal entries
  • Accounts receivable and accounts payable data, including invoices, bills, and payment records
  • Customer and vendor records
  • Chart of accounts and general ledger data

The Application operates in read-only mode. It does not create, modify, or delete any data within QuickBooks Online.

3. How We Use Accessed Data

Data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used solely for the following internal business purposes:

  • Financial reporting and analysis
  • Reconciliation and accounting workflow support
  • Internal dashboards and management reporting

We do not use QuickBooks Online data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the internal financial operations of Ready Rebound, Inc.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, license, or share QuickBooks Online data with any third party, except as required by law. Specifically:

  • Data is not shared with external vendors, partners, or service providers for their independent use.
  • Data may be disclosed to legal or regulatory authorities if required by applicable law or court order.
  • Data may be shared with Intuit as necessary for API connectivity and technical support.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Any QuickBooks Online data processed or cached by the Application is stored within Ready Rebound's internal systems in the United States. Data is retained in accordance with Ready Rebound's standard financial record retention policies, generally seven years from the date of the underlying transaction.

Access to stored data is restricted to authorized Ready Rebound personnel with a legitimate business need.

6. Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect accessed data, including:

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication for all QuickBooks Online API connections
  • Secure storage of API credentials — not hardcoded in application code
  • Access controls limiting Application use to authorized internal personnel
  • Transport Layer Security (TLS) for all data in transit

7. Authorized Users

The Application is accessible only to authorized employees and contractors of Ready Rebound, Inc. who have been granted access by a system administrator. Unauthorized access is prohibited.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at https://zeggy418.github.io/rr-qbo-policies/privacy-policy.html.
